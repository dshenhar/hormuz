HORMUZ DEFENDER

Hormuz Defender is a free browser-based arcade shooter inspired by the geopolitical importance of the Strait of Hormuz — one of the world's most critical oil shipping chokepoints. Pilot an F-22 stealth jet, intercept waves of Shahed kamikaze drones, protect friendly oil tankers, and keep the strait open for international shipping.

HOW TO PLAY

  • Move — Arrow keys or WASD (desktop) / Virtual joystick (mobile)
  • Fire — Space bar (hold for auto-fire) / FIRE button on mobile
  • Bomb — B key / BOMB button — clears all enemies on screen
  • Pause — P key / PAUSE button
  • ⚠ Do NOT shoot friendly tankers — you lose a life and strait health

GAME FEATURES

  • Endless wave-based progression — each wave is faster and harder
  • 3 enemy types: Fast Drones, Patrol Drones, and Heavy Drones
  • Boss battles every 5th wave
  • Kharg Island fires SAM missiles — shoot them down for bonus points
  • Power-ups: Triple Shot, Extra Bomb, Extra Life, Speed Boost, Israel Shield
  • Friendly tankers to protect — escort them safely through the strait
  • Sea mines dropped by enemies that block the water lane
  • Strait Health meter — let too many enemies through and it's game over
  • Retro arcade aesthetic with CRT scanline effect
  • Full sound effects generated in real time via Web Audio API

ABOUT THE STRAIT OF HORMUZ

The Strait of Hormuz is a narrow waterway between Iran and Oman connecting the Persian Gulf to the Gulf of Oman. Approximately 20% of the world's petroleum and 25% of global liquefied natural gas pass through this strait every day, making it one of the most strategically important maritime chokepoints on Earth.

Key locations featured in the game include Bandar Abbas (Iran's main naval base), Kharg Island (Iran's primary oil export terminal), Musandam Peninsula (Oman's exclave), and the narrow strait itself — only 33 km wide at its narrowest point.

TIPS & STRATEGY

  • Save bombs for boss waves or when overwhelmed by enemies
  • Collect the Shield power-up before boss fights — it absorbs one hit
  • Shoot SAM missiles from Kharg Island for +150 points each
  • Stay near the center of the water lane for maximum maneuverability
  • Triple Shot power-up dramatically increases your firepower — prioritize it
  • Watch the Strait Health bar — enemies and enemy bullets damage tankers
