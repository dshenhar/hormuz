Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Welcome to Hormuz Defender (hormuz-defender.vercel.app). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit and play our free browser game.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Hormuz Defender does not require registration or any personal information to play. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information directly.

The following data may be collected automatically:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (desktop or mobile)
  • Approximate geographic region (country level, via ad networks)
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • IP address (processed by third-party services, not stored by us)

2. COOKIES AND TRACKING

Our site uses cookies primarily through third-party advertising and analytics services. These cookies help serve relevant advertisements and measure site traffic.

  • Google AdSense — displays advertisements and may use cookies to show personalized ads based on your browsing history.
  • Google Analytics — may be used to understand how visitors use the site (aggregated, anonymized data only).

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings or by using the NAI opt-out tool.

3. GOOGLE ADSENSE AND ADVERTISING

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this site. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on prior visits to this and other websites. You may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie by visiting the Google advertising opt-out page.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet. Learn more about how Google uses data at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

4. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

Any data collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Displaying relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • Improving site performance and user experience
  • Understanding general traffic patterns (aggregate, non-personal)

We do not sell, trade, or transfer any information to third parties other than the advertising and analytics services described above.

5. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Hormuz Defender is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through this site, please contact us so we can remove it.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have certain rights regarding your personal data under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Personalized advertising requires your consent, which Google AdSense will request via a consent banner when you visit the site from these regions.

This site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. DATA SECURITY
